# Changelog

## 1.0.0

Initial validated Windows binary release of IntegraDWExposureModel.

- Deterministic and probabilistic drinking-water exposure simulation.
- Sparse drinking-event model for direct water ingestion.
- Fixed and stochastic chemical concentration inputs.
- Event, hourly, daily, and exposure-period outputs.
- Disk-backed raw event and hourly outputs.
- External oral-exposure exports for downstream PBPK models.
